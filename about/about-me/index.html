About Me

Posted on Jul 11, 2022
2 minutes read

I am a 4th-year student in École Polytechnique in France, enrolled in the “Cycle Ingénieur” Program. I am also a 2nd-year Master student in Shanghai Jiao Tong University as part of my double-degree program supervised by Ya Zhang. I am currently working as a research intern at Sea AI Lab in Singapore, under the guidance of Lin Min and Wang Yangzihao. I am working on a project that accelerates deep learning compiler (XLA) optimization and other interesting stuffs. Before that, I was an intern in OpenAI and participated in building an AI that proves mathematics theorem, under the guidance of Stanislas Polu and Jesse Michael Han.

Before École Polytechnique, I obtained my Bachelor degree in a Sino-French prépa institute in Shanghai Jiao Tong University, where I received intense training of mathematics and physics in French for the entrance to the Grand École.

My education gives me training of general background of computer science and mathematics before getting into deep learning. Thanks to Oana Balalau and Ioana Manolescu, I had my first contact with research in machine learning in INRIA late 2019. I always believe that a good and solid research project in the field of deep learning contains both nice ideas and good engineering efforts. This thought is furthured shaped along my trajectory in Stockly, OpenAI & Sea AI Lab, where I saw and learned a lot about people building cool things in Rust, Lean, C++.
